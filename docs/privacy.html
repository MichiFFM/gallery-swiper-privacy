Privacy Policy Data Collection GallerySwiper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All photo processing happens locally on your device. Photo Library Access Your photos remain on your device at all times. The app reads them locally to display them for review. No photos, thumbnails, or metadata are ever uploaded or shared with any server. Photo Deletion Android: When you confirm deletion, photos are moved to the system trash. Nothing is deleted until you explicitly confirm. iOS (and others): When you confirm deletion, photos are permanently removed from your device. Nothing is deleted until you explicitly confirm. Advertising GallerySwiper may display ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymous device identifiers to serve non-personalized ads. No personally identifiable information is involved. You can remove ads permanently via the in-app purchase. In-App Purchases Purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. GallerySwiper does not receive or store any payment information. Session Data Review progress (which photos you've swiped) is saved locally on your device only. It is automatically deleted when you complete a session. Contact Questions about this policy?
